Why This Background?

My favorite season has to be Autumn. I love the colors and the weather. When I saw this picture it reminded me of the first time driving through the mid-west during the end of fall. It was the first time I had ever seen such natural colors in person. It was early in the morning and I was on one of my adventures driving from Los Angeles, California to a little town in Ohio. I saw the sun come up and all I could see where beautiful hill sides, farms and big gorgeous trees and so many colors, I was awestruck! It really was an incredible sight and that's why I picked this image.
