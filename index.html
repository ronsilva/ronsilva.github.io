Why This Background?

My favorite season has to be Autumn. I love the colors and like the weather. When I saw this picture it reminded me of times of walking with my family on a cool evening, enjoying a hot-coco.   As the year-end nears, lights go up all around, the trees once lush with leaves, are bare, awaiting the winter months, and the streets filled with their colorful offerings; those are the sights that impress me the most.
